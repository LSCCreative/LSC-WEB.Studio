Brand Voice
The LSC Creative Voice
Cinematic.
Direct. Real.
LSC Creative doesn't use marketing language. We write and speak the way a great documentary filmmaker thinks — with precision, without ego, and always in service of the truth. Every word earns its place.
01
Direct
Say it once, say it well. No padding, no corporate softening, no hedge words. We trust the reader's intelligence.
In practice
"We make brand films." Not "We offer comprehensive video production solutions."
02
Cinematic
Language that evokes craft and atmosphere. We borrow from the world of film — not the world of marketing decks.
In practice
"Shot on location. No scripts." Not "Authentic, human-centred content."
03
Confident
We don't qualify. We don't ask permission. The work speaks — our words frame it without apology.
In practice
"This is our best work yet." Not "We're proud to share what we feel is a strong project."
04
Human
Premium doesn't mean cold. Behind every brand is a person with a real story. We write warmly, not stiffly.
In practice
"Tell us your story." Not "Submit your project brief for evaluation."

Write like this
→
Short sentences. Hard stops.
→
Active voice. Subject, verb, done.
→
Specific details over vague claims.
→
One idea per sentence.
→
Fragments are fine. They land harder.
✕
Never write like this
→
Buzzwords: "world-class", "synergy", "ecosystem".
→
Passive voice: "films are crafted by our team".
→
Hedging: "we feel", "we believe", "hopefully".
→
Superlatives without proof: "the best", "leading".
→
Exclamation marks. Ever.

Rules for AI models
