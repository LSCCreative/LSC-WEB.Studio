How We Work

The Process.

Three phases.
Zero compromise.
01

Pre-production.

The quality of your project is in the details. I take planning very seriously no matter the scope of the project — depending on what your story needs, scripting, storyboarding, short listing, location scouting and finding the right talent can mean the difference between a clear strong message or a lost audience. I love planning because I'm dictating the end result before anybody gets on set. It's exciting to come to a vision with my clients so that everybody is on the same page.

02

Production.

Where we hit record on set. What makes LSC Creative different is the standard between executing a single shoot or an ongoing production stays the same — the lighting, cameras and over-caffeinated crew members are coming together to put planning into action.

03

Post-production.

Where the magic happens — the rigorous work of editing. Managing the refinement of unique footage and spoken words, compiled under the light of professional colour science and sound design to deliver elevated media.
