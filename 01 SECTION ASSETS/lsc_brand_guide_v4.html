Brand Voice
The LSC Creative Voice
Cinematic.
Direct. Real.
LSC Creative doesn't use marketing language. We write and speak the way a great documentary filmmaker thinks — with precision, without ego, and always in service of the truth. Every word earns its place.
01
Direct
Say it once, say it well. No padding, no corporate softening, no hedge words. We trust the reader's intelligence.
In practice
"We make brand films." Not "We offer comprehensive video production solutions."
02
Cinematic
Language that evokes craft and atmosphere. We borrow from the world of film — not the world of marketing decks.
In practice
"Shot on location. No scripts." Not "Authentic, human-centred content."
03
Confident
We don't qualify. We don't ask permission. The work speaks — our words frame it without apology.
In practice
"This is our best work yet." Not "We're proud to share what we feel is a strong project."
04
Human
Premium doesn't mean cold. Behind every brand is a person with a real story. We write warmly, not stiffly.
In practice
"Tell us your story." Not "Submit your project brief for evaluation."

Responsive Architecture — Immutable Laws
01
Desktop Preservation Law
All base CSS selectors are reserved exclusively for the desktop experience (viewport > 992px). You are strictly prohibited from altering, refactoring, or deleting existing desktop styles when adjusting mobile layouts.
All mobile overrides → isolated inside @media (max-width: 992px) or @media (max-width: 768px)
Appended to the absolute bottom of the stylesheet. Never interspersed.
02
Navigation Collapse Law
On screens ≤ 768px, inline text nav links are prohibited due to horizontal spatial crowding. They must collapse into a .mobile-menu-toggle button with a hairline SVG icon. The drawer uses .is-active to reveal via slide-down glassmorphic overlay.
Desktop: nav links inline · Mobile ≤ 768px: hamburger toggle → .nav-links.absolute-nav.is-active
03
Touch Gesture Paradigm
Desktop hover triggers do not exist on touch screens. Any interaction relying on :hover must fall back to a native touch gesture or become permanently visible on phone viewports.
@media (hover: none) and (pointer: coarse) { .project-tile:hover .project-info-overlay { opacity: 0; } }
04
Horizontal → Vertical Feed Law
Complex horizontal scroll pinning must be dismantled on mobile. Transform horizontal tracks into a vertical cinematic feed or an optimised touch-scroll track. Child elements reset from absolute to relative positioning, bounded to max-width: 100%.
@media (max-width: 768px) { .film-strip-container { display: contents !important; } .film-frames-grid { overflow-x: auto !important; -webkit-overflow-scrolling: touch; } }
